## Template Download Workflow (`v3.0.0-beta1`)

WP3 Pro adds a faster template acquisition flow so operators can download and prepare PhishPortal templates directly from the interface.

### What improved

- Centralized template download screen
- Faster onboarding for campaigns and lab tests
- Better consistency between downloaded template assets and local template catalog

### How to use

1. Open the PhishPortal template manager.
2. Go to the download templates section.
3. Select the template you want to import.
4. Confirm download and wait for catalog refresh.
5. Apply the template in your campaign flow.
